Good To Know

Frequently asked questions

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive by 6:00 PM for the Kabbalat Panim. The Chuppah begins at 7:00 PM and, as it's held before sunset, we'll be starting on time.

What is a Kabbalat Panim?

The reception before the ceremony, where guests greet and celebrate with the bride and groom separately, often with food, drinks, and music.

What is a Chuppah?

The wedding canopy under which the ceremony takes place — symbolizing the home the couple will build together.

What should I wear?

Semi-formal attire. Keep in mind it's an outdoor garden venue — you may want comfortable footwear for grass and garden paths.

Is there parking?

Yes — there is free parking at Ein Hemed. See the Directions section for maps and navigation links.
